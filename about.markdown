## About Crimson Sun

This channel shares my guitar chords for anime music. If you'd like me to write chords for any anime song, please inbox me on YouTube or comment on any of my videos.

### A Note About My Chords

My chords aren't 100% accurate - they represent my personal feeling and interpretation of the music, so they're relative to my perception. I look forward to receiving your feedback about my chords to help improve my skills.

Have fun! ^^

### What to Expect

- **Anime Music Focus**: Dedicated to chords and arrangements for anime songs
- **Personal Interpretation**: Chords based on my musical feeling and interpretation
- **Community Requests**: Open to suggestions for new anime songs to transcribe
- **Continuous Improvement**: Always learning and refining based on feedback

### Get Involved

Feel free to:
- Request anime songs you'd like chords for
- Share your feedback on existing chord arrangements
- Suggest improvements or alternative interpretations
- Enjoy the music and have fun playing!
